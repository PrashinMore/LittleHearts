Classes for Your Kids

PLAYGROUP (Age 2-2.5)

In Playgroup, toddlers explore, play, and learn in a warm and stimulating environment. Through age-appropriate activities, they develop essential skills like communication, socialization, and motor skills. Our experienced educators create a nurturing atmosphere where every child feels safe, valued, and encouraged to discover.

NURSERY (Age 3)

Nursery lays the foundation for academic success while nurturing creativity. Children engage in hands-on activities, storytelling, music, and art to develop early literacy and numeracy skills. With a playbased curriculum, we foster a love for learning in every child.

JUNIOR KG (Age 4)

Junior KG prepares children for kindergarten with interactive lessons and outdoor exploration. They develop cognitive, social, and emotional skills to thrive in school and beyond

SENIOR KG (Age 5)

Senior KG refines academic skills and prepares for formal education. With a focus on literacy, numeracy, and critical thinking, children develop confidence and independence. Through project-based learning, they cultivate a lifelong love for learning.
